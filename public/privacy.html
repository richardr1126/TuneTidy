TuneTidy's Privacy Policy

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your information when using our services.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from our users. All data, including but not limited to the Spotify OAuth token for the user, top artists, and top tracks, is stored locally on the user's device.

Storage and Processing

To provide our services, we utilize both the browser's local storage and device storage to store non-sensitive data, including:

  • The Spotify OAuth token for the user.
  • Users' top artists.
  • Users' top tracks.

It is important to note that this data remains on your device and is never transmitted or shared with us.

Third-party Services

The iOS App's AI Playlist Cover Art generation uses OpenAI's Dalle Model. Your generation prompts are sent to them and may be used by them to improve their generation models. When you create your first playlist image cover art a random id is generated for your deivce, your prompt and your random id are the only data sent to OpenAI. View their privacy policy..

We do not share any other data with third parties. Our commitment to your privacy ensures that your information is neither shared nor transferred to any external organizations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. It is advised that users regularly review the Privacy Policy to stay informed about any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at privacy@tunetidy.com.
